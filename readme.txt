= HOW DOES VOUCHKICK WORK? =

Install it with this Wordpress plugin, and sign in.

Build your kits (widgets) you want to show on your website. 

Choose 'Where' on your website to display your kits.

Choose "When' to trigger your kits to show.

Personalize each kit to match your brand. 

Connect your kits to 1,100+ apps to automate actions, notifications, conversions, and more.

== Installation ==

1. Install Vouchkick either via the WordPress.org plugin repository or by uploading the files to your server.
2. Activate your Vouchkick account.
3. Login using the wordpress plugin.
4. Start creating your kits.
